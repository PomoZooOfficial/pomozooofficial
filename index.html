PomoZoo Privacy Policy

Effective date: Last updated 12.04.2025

1. No Personal Data Collected

PomoZoo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive data. The app is designed to function entirely without requiring user information such as names, emails, or location data.

2. Local Device Storage

All app-related data, such as settings or timers, is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device or is shared with anyone, including the developers.

3. User Control

You have full control over your app data. Data can be reset or cleared directly within the app at any time.

4. No Tracking or Cookies

PomoZoo does not use cookies, tracking tools, or analytics technologies in the app or on our website.

5. Legal Compliance

While PomoZoo does not collect data, we aim to comply with privacy regulations such as GDPR and CCPA through complete data minimization by design.

6. Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted on this website. We encourage you to check periodically for changes.
